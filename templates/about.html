EDD Bookings is a premium, third-party extension for Easy Digital Downloads, brought to you by RebelCode.

Your services are currently listed as "Downloads" in your dashboard. To make a Download a Service, simply enable bookings within the Edit Download page.

To display the booking wizard to your clients, add the EDD Bookings shortcode to any post, page or other area on your site: [eddbk]
